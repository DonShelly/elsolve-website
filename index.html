§ 01

What we believe.

i

Generic AI tools cannot solve enterprise problems. Context fragmentation, inconsistent output quality at scale, and knowledge trapped inside individuals require bespoke infrastructure — not another SaaS subscription.

ii

Great systems begin with the organisation, not the model. We start from how a team actually operates, map the leverage points, then build to fit — not to approximate.

iii

Your data stays under your control. The system runs on your own infrastructure. ELSOLVE provides the build and ongoing support — you own the asset.

iv

We ship. An engagement that never reaches production is a consultancy deck. We design, build, and hand over systems that keep working — and scale as the organisation grows.

§ 02

How we work.

01

Discovery

A structured diagnostic: we understand the organisation, the team structure, the highest-leverage problem to solve first, and what a useful first deployment actually looks like.

02

Scope

We produce a scoping note and Statement of Work — documenting the proposed deployment, agent architecture, integrations, timeline, and expected outcomes. One page. Unambiguous.

03

Build

We design and deploy the system inside your own infrastructure — typically two to five AI agents working together, configured around your workflows, terminology, knowledge base, and operating model.

04

Hand over

You own the system. Full documentation, team onboarding, and a structured support period. We remain available for optimisation and expansion as your use case evolves.

The people using these systems are strategists, account directors, executives, and operators — not engineers. We build for real-world usage patterns: natural language, minimal setup friction, and results that compound quietly over time.

The ELSOLVE design principle.
